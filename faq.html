FAQ

Answers to common questions about our residential flooring services.

If you don’t see your question here, contact us and we’ll help you plan the right solution for your home.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you work on small projects?
Yes. We handle projects of all sizes, from single rooms to full-home flooring.
How long does installation take?
Most installations take between one and five days, depending on materials and scope.
Do you help choose materials?
Absolutely. We guide you through options based on budget, durability, and design.
Do you offer warranties?
Warranty details depend on materials and project scope and are provided in writing.
Is your service residential only?
Yes. We specialize exclusively in residential properties.
Do you move furniture?
We can coordinate a moving plan based on your space and timeline. If you’d like full-service prep, tell us and we’ll include it in the estimate.
Will there be dust during sanding?
We use professional dust-control systems to keep your home cleaner during sanding and refinishing. We’ll also protect adjacent areas as needed.
Can you install flooring over existing floors?
Sometimes. It depends on the existing material, height transitions, and the condition of the subfloor. We’ll confirm the best approach during the consultation.
How do you protect my home during installation?
We use floor protection, containment when needed, and daily cleanup so your home stays safe and livable throughout the project.
Do you dispose of old flooring?
Removal and disposal can be included in your estimate. Let us know what’s in place today and we’ll plan the right scope.

Still have questions?

Tell us about your space and we’ll recommend the best next step.
