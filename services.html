FAQ
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — we provide free on-site estimates for residential projects. We’ll review measurements, materials, and scope so you get a clear plan and price.
How long does installation take?
Timelines vary by material and square footage. Most residential installs range from 1–5 days, and we’ll confirm the schedule before work begins.
Do you help with furniture and prep?
We can coordinate prep guidance and moving plans based on your space. If you’d like full-service prep, tell us and we’ll include it in the estimate.
Can you match existing floors?
We do our best to match tone, sheen, and plank profile. For sand & finish projects, we can often blend new and existing areas for a seamless look.

Ready to upgrade your floors?

Tell us about your space and goals — we’ll recommend the right materials and a clear plan for installation.
